About features of this website

  • "AI sentiment analysis of comments"
  • Collects 25 comments from the comment section of a given IMDB web site, starting with the newest one. The comments are identified by NLTK using negative and positive words, and three graphs are displayed
  • 1,A graph showing the percentage of rating scores given to the film.
  • 2. A graph showing the percentage of negative and positive comments identified by sentiment analysis.
  • 3. A graph showing the agreement between the ratings voluntarily given by the user and the ratings identified by the sentiment analysis function.

How to use

  • 1. Click the button "Run Scraping" at the bottom
  • 2. The result screen will automatically change to the result screen.
  • 3. Check the graph of the three results displayed there
